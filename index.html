About Me

I’m Edwin O. Ouko, a software and machine learning engineer based in Seattle, WA. I work on cloud infrastructure and applied ML, and I’m currently at Microsoft on Azure Local.


I earned both my MEng and BS in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from MIT. My interest spans machine learning, distributed systems, Kubernetes, and embedded systems, and I enjoy collaborating on projects that convert research to real-life applications.


Outside of engineering, I’m interested in STEM outreach initiatives, economics, and public policy.
